About the Project

Eduvoice
Revolutionizing Education with Voice-to-Text Technology and AI Chatbots
What is Eduvoice?
Brief description of Eduvoice - a project that transforms spoken words into text and features a chatbot for interactive discussions.
Why Eduvoice?
  • To improve accessibility for students with disabilities
  • To enhance note-taking efficiency
  • To provide interactive learning experiences
